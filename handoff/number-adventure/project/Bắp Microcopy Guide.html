Tone Rules

Child Mode — Warm & Playful

  • Short sentences — 6 words or fewer when possible
  • First person from Bắp: "I" / "We" / "Let's"
  • Never say "wrong" or "fail" — say "almost" or "try again"
  • Use exclamation marks sparingly (1 per screen max)
  • No academic tone — "find the number", not "identify the numeral"
  • Always encouraging — every outcome feels okay

Parent Mode — Clear & Helpful

  • Vietnamese as primary, clean and modern phrasing
  • Respectful, not condescending — parents are partners
  • Specific labels — "15 phút / ngày", not "a little each day"
  • Explain the why briefly — "Khi hết thời gian, bé sẽ thấy màn hình nghỉ ngơi nhẹ nhàng"
  • No guilt or pressure — "Bé đang tiến bộ", not "Bé chưa đạt mục tiêu"
