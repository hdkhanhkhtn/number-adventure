# Product Overview

## Vision

Make learning numbers joyful for young children by turning practice into an adventure — with a lovable mascot, rewarding progression, and audio-first interaction that works even before a child can read.

## Problem Statement

Children aged 3–7 need repeated, varied exposure to numbers to build number sense. Traditional worksheets are boring. Existing apps are either too complex, ad-supported, or not designed for pre-readers. Screen time is happening anyway — this app makes it genuinely educational.

## Solution

**Bap Number Adventure** — a mobile-first game app where children explore a world map, play 5 different number mini-games, earn stickers, and build streaks. Parents get a dashboard to track daily progress without interrupting the child's experience.

## Target Users

### Primary: Children (Age 3–7)

- Pre-readers or early readers
- Need large touch targets (small hands)
- Guided by audio — cannot rely on text labels
- Short attention span — sessions max 5–10 minutes
- Motivated by visual rewards (stickers, celebrations)

### Secondary: Parents

- Want assurance child is learning, not just playing
- Need control over session length and content
- Value privacy: no ads, no external links, no accounts required

## Constraints

- **No accounts / backend** — all data local (localStorage) for MVP
- **No ads** — fully offline-capable
- **Child-safe** — COPPA-aware, no external links, parent gate
- **Mobile-first** — optimized for 375px–430px portrait
